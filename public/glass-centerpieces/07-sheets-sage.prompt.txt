A sculptural composition of large translucent glass sheets folded and layered at sharp clean angles, like origami caught mid-fold. Sage, emerald, jade, chartreuse, and ivory tones. Sharp polished edges where sheets meet. Studio raking light from one side reveals each layer's translucency and edge-sheen. Bottom-left of frame is empty for typography. Tara Donovan material-study aesthetic, museum object photography, subsurface scattering visible at the folds. 4K editorial sculpture.
