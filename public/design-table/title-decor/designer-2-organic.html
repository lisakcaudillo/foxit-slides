Design Table · Title Slide Decoration

Designer 2 — Organic / Atmospheric

Six subtle vector decorations that extend the PM's beloved "light rays" cover energy into a calmer, broader vocabulary: flowing ribbons, topographic contours, an aurora gradient-mesh glow, drifting bokeh, a fine grain wash, and an abstract fluid field. All are pure SVG/CSS, theme-tinted via 1–2 palette colors at 8–18% opacity, sit behind the title, and render independent of the photo toggle. The title always stays the legible hero.

Dark theme · violet / purple
Light theme · warm editorial (same motifs, tinted to palette)

How they stay subtle & theme-adaptive: every motif draws with the theme's tint color (--tint / --tint2) and a neutral (--neutral) at 8–18% opacity, never a hardcoded hue — so the exact same SVG re-tints from violet-on-dark to warm-ochre-on-light with one CSS variable swap, mirroring the currentColor + dark pattern in CoverArt.tsx. Strokes are hairline (0.4–1px), fills sit under 0.18, and all decoration is anchored to edges/corners away from the title block, so it reads as atmosphere behind the words rather than competition with them.
