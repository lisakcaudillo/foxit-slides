Design Table · Title / Cover Decorations · Designer 3

Editorial & Minimal

Six restrained, premium-print decorations for title slides. Each is pure vector (SVG/CSS), theme-tinted through 1–2 palette colors at low opacity, and decorative-only (pointer-events: none). The title stays the hero; the decoration is an accent in a field of negative space. All six render independent of the photo toggle, and adapt to dark and light themes via a single currentColor-style ink token.
