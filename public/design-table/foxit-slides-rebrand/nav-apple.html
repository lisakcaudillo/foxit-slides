Material, not paint. The rail is translucent vibrancy — it tints to the wallpaper behind the window, so it reads as glass.
One accent, used twice. The violet appears only on the selected row and the primary button. Everything else is neutral.
Soft selection. A full-width rounded-rect tint — no left-bar, no glow. Icon and label shift to the accent.
Liquid-Glass action. "New Presentation" is a translucent glass capsule with a specular top edge, not a saturated slab.
Deference. The brand gradient survives only on the logo; the chrome stays quiet so the decks are the hero.
Hairlines for depth. Separation comes from 7%-black hairlines and the material — never drop-shadows in the rail.
