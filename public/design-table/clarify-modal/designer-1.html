  • Lavender-wash continuity. Backdrop dim is a violet-tinted radial (rgba(107,63,160,0.18) → slate-900 at 42%) instead of flat black, so the kit .bg-compose-wash reads through. The modal feels like it lifted off the home page, not parachuted in from another product.
  • Surface = kit .glass-card + scaled-up elevation. Same rgba(255,255,255,0.85) + backdrop-blur recipe from globals.css line 416, with a layered shadow stack (1px hairline + 60px violet bloom + 24px violet halo + inset highlight). Radius 20px — one step up from the kit's card radius of 16px to keep proportion at modal scale; chips stay at the kit's 10px.
  • Premium "selected" chip state. Not a border swap. Combines (a) a 135° kit-pastel gradient tint at ~18% opacity, (b) violet-deep #3B2856 text, (c) a 3px violet-600 outer halo plus an inset highlight, and (d) a check pip that fades in. Reads as "lit from within" rather than "bordered" — matches the kit's layering language.
  • Header accent strip. 4px kit-pastel gradient (pink → lavender → sky) at 85% opacity directly under the radius. Anchors the surface as AI without resorting to a heavy violet header. Pairs with a violet-600 uppercase eyebrow ("COMPOSE AI") for explicit attribution per P4 (AI provenance).
